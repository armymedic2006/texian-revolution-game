# Victory Conditions

## Victory Points

All victory points begin under Texian control.

Major victory points:

1. Chapel
2. Long Barrack
3. Main Plaza / Courtyard
4. Main Gate
5. North Wall
6. Palisade
7. Convento / Low Barrack area
8. Key artillery battery position

All victory points have equal value.

Victory points are objective zones only. They do not provide automatic tactical bonuses.

## Mexican Victory

The Mexican army wins immediately if all Texian defenders are killed.

The Mexican army may also win by controlling all victory points before the turn limit expires.

To capture a Texian-controlled victory point:

- Mexican units must be the only eligible units inside the victory point radius.
- They must hold it uncontested for 3 consecutive turns.
- If any Texian unit enters the radius, the timer resets.

## Texian Victory

Texian defenders win immediately if all Mexican units are killed or routed off the map.

Texian defenders also win immediately if Santa Anna is routed.

Texian defenders win at the turn limit if they control at least one victory point.

To recapture a Mexican-controlled victory point:

- Texian units must be the only eligible units inside the victory point radius.
- They must hold it uncontested for 2 consecutive turns.
- If any Mexican unit enters the radius, the timer resets.

## Victory Point Contesting

Texian Broken/Routed units can contest victory points.

Mexican Broken/Routed units cannot contest or control victory points because they retreat toward fallback/map-exit points.

Officers alone cannot capture, recapture, or contest victory points.

Artillery crews count as eligible units for victory point control.

Cavalry counts as eligible units for victory point control.

## Victory Point Visibility

- Current victory point ownership is visible.
- Capture progress is hidden.
- Players are always notified when the enemy starts capturing one of their victory points.
- The notification identifies the specific victory point only if it is within line of sight.
- Otherwise, the player receives a generic warning.
- Victory point radius is always visible.

## Turn Limits

Single-player vs AI:

- Turn limit varies by difficulty.

Hot-seat and non-AI modes:

- Turn limit is player-selectable.

Turns are abstract gameplay rounds and do not represent fixed historical minutes.
