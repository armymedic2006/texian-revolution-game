# Named Officers

## General Officer Rules

Version 1 uses named officers only.

No generic officers.

Every named officer is an individual on-map unit.

Officers can:

- Move
- Shoot
- Fight in melee
- Use passive command effects
- Use active command abilities
- Rally units

Officers can be killed or routed.

Officers cannot be wounded in version 1.

Named officers have greater morale impact than generic officers would have.

Officer morale impact scope depends on the officer’s importance and abilities.

## Officer Action Economy

- Passive command effects apply automatically when conditions are met.
- Active command abilities consume the officer’s action.
- Officers may fight normally when not using an active command ability.

## Officer Survivability

Officers are harder to kill than ordinary individual soldiers.

They receive:

- Higher defense value
- Higher chance to avoid lethal hits

## Officer Identity Visibility

Opponent identification depends on:

- Distance
- Line of sight
- Scouting ability
- Recognition conditions

All officers use the same identification rules.

Famous officers are not automatically easier to identify.

## Mexican Officers

All Mexican officers have:

- Sword
- Pistol

### Santa Anna

Santa Anna is an on-map Mexican officer.

Abilities:

- Large Mexican morale aura
- Increases Mexican movement speed
- Increases Mexican willingness to continue attacking despite casualties
- When within Texian line of sight and close enough to be attacked, Santa Anna increases Texian morale and combat effectiveness

Santa Anna risk/reward rules:

- If Santa Anna is killed:
  - Texians gain +25% defensive bonus
  - Texians gain +25% melee bonus
  - Mexican forces suffer a major morale penalty
- If Santa Anna is routed:
  - Texians immediately win
- Santa Anna cannot be captured in version 1
- Santa Anna cannot be wounded in version 1

## Texian Officers

### William B. Travis

Loadout:

- Double-barreled shotgun

Special rule:

- Shotgun has increased lethality at close range compared with musket or rifle

### James Bowie

Loadout:

- Two pistols
- Bowie knife

Special rule:

- Bowie knife is 6x more combat effective/deadly than a normal sword or Bowie knife

### David “Davy” Crockett

Loadout:

- Old Betsy rifle
- Bowie knife

Special rules:

- Old Betsy has 4x accuracy compared with regular rifles
- Old Betsy has 2x range compared with regular rifles

### Juan Seguín

Historical Mode:

- Off-map historical/contextual support only

Alternate-History Mode:

- On-map officer

Abilities:

- Improves Tejano Volunteer morale
- Improves Tejano Volunteer spotting/fog-of-war detection
- Improves movement/scouting
- Can reveal hidden Mexican units within line of sight
- Can rally Texian or Tejano units
- Has rifle + Bowie knife combat bonuses

Reveal ability:

- Passive spotting bonus
- Active reveal ability
- Both require line of sight
- Active reveal shares information only with friendly units within Juan Seguín’s command radius

## Legendary Abilities

Legendary named-officer weapons and abilities are always part of game balance.

There is no realism toggle in version 1.
